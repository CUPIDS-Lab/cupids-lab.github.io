Since 2025, federal and state agencies have removed, restricted, or quietly edited hundreds of public datasets. If a page you relied on is gone — or you suspect the numbers changed — we can help: recovering data from web archives and caches, comparing versions to document what was altered, and preserving a citable copy so your reporting stands.

This is core to our mission. See the [At-Risk Public Data Archive](/projects/at-risk-archive/) and our [data documentation guides](/resources/data-documentation-guides/), which record each dataset's provenance and limitations.

[Tell the desk what disappeared](/get-involved/#desk), or use our [secure channels](/get-involved/#secure) for anything sensitive.
