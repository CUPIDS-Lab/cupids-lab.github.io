A finding only lands if your audience can see it. We design charts, maps, and graphics that make the point honestly and clearly — choosing the right form, labeling it plainly, and avoiding the distortions that mislead readers.

We can deliver a finished, publication-ready graphic or hand you a reusable template, and we'll explain the choices so the visual stays faithful to the data.

See the [CUPIDS design guide](/resources/cupids-design-guide/) and our [resources](/resources/), then [bring your data to the desk](/get-involved/#desk).
