Reports, court filings, FOIA responses, scanned forms — the information you need is often locked inside hundreds of pages of PDF. We extract it: parsing tables, running OCR on scans, and structuring the result into clean spreadsheets or databases ready for analysis.

Whether it's one dense report or a folder of thousands, we'll turn documents into data — and document how we did it, so your numbers hold up to scrutiny.

Browse our [tutorials & how-tos](/resources/tutorials-howtos/) and [tooling & code](/resources/tooling-code/), or [describe your documents to the desk](/get-involved/#desk). Working with sensitive material? Use our [secure channels](/get-involved/#secure) instead.
