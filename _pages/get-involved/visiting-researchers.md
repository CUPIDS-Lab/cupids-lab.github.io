Visiting Researchers bring professional experience into the lab and carry new data capacity back out. These are one-semester or full-year appointments for **mid-career practitioners** — working journalists, lawyers, policy researchers, civic technologists, and nonprofit leaders — who want dedicated time to pursue a data-intensive project or deepen their technical skills.

The call is broad: whatever your beat, sector, or method, if your work serves the public interest, you're welcome. Appointments come with workspace and university access — libraries, computing, and data resources — plus collaboration with our student teams and faculty.

You'll leave with a concrete project, new methods, and a standing connection to the lab's network, including the [help desk](/get-involved/#request-help) and our [environmental journalism partnership](/projects/cej/). Stipend and support details are to be confirmed.

Interested? [Start a conversation](/get-involved/#inquire) or email [brian.keegan@colorado.edu](mailto:brian.keegan@colorado.edu).
