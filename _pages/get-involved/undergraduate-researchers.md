Undergraduate Researchers are where the pipeline begins — competitive, mentored positions for CU undergraduates who want to learn **real data science skills** while contributing to work that helps Colorado's newsrooms, nonprofits, and communities.

You don't need to be a computer scientist, or to have any prior experience. We welcome students from **every major and background**; curiosity, care, and a willingness to learn matter more than any particular résumé. You'll be mentored by graduate researchers and faculty, build a portfolio of public-facing projects, and find a pathway toward graduate study and professional practice.

Expect hands-on training in finding, cleaning, analyzing, and visualizing public data — the same skills our [resources](/resources/) and [help desk](/get-involved/#request-help) put to work every day. Compensation details are to be confirmed.

Curious? [Introduce yourself](/get-involved/#inquire) or email [brian.keegan@colorado.edu](mailto:brian.keegan@colorado.edu).
