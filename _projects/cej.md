In partnership with the [Center for Environmental Journalism](/partners/center-environmental-journalism/), CUPIDS is identifying, prioritizing, and archiving the federal environmental datasets most critical to Colorado journalism — and equipping reporters to use them when primary sources go dark. Supported by the 2026 de Castro Research Award, with {% person hong-tien-vu %}, Director, CEJ.

A full project page — datasets, methods, and findings — is on the way. Until then, [browse resources](/resources/) or [request help](/get-involved/).
