In partnership with the **Center for Environmental Journalism**, CUPIDS is
identifying, prioritizing, and archiving the federal environmental datasets
most critical to Colorado journalism — and equipping reporters to use them when
primary sources go dark. Supported by the 2026 de Castro Research Award, with
Hong Tien Vu, Director, CEJ.

A full project page — datasets, methods, and findings — is on the way. Until
then, [browse resources](/resources/) or [request help](/get-involved/).
