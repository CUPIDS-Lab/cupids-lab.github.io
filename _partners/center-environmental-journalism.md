The Center for Environmental Journalism (CEJ) at CU Boulder trains journalists, supports environmental reporting, and runs the Ted Scripps Fellowships and the Water Desk.

CUPIDS partners with CEJ on the 2026 [de Castro Research Award](/projects/cej/) to identify, archive, and document the federal environmental datasets Colorado journalism depends on — and to put them back in reporters' hands when primary sources go dark.
